Bio

  • I am currently a researcher at the Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) under the supervision of Prof. Hui Xiong. I received my B.S. degree in Mathematics from USTC in 2017. I have also spent time at Talent Intelligent Center, Baidu Inc. as a Research Intern from 2017 to 2022 under the supervision of Dr. Hengshu Zhu.
  • My general research interest includes data mining and machine learning, focusing on generative models, variational inference, and their data intensive applications.
